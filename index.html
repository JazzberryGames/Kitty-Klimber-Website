What is Kitty Klimber?

Kitty Klimber is a fun, fast paced game for cat lovers everywhere. Play it because it's incredibly cute.

You play as a cat and race against the notorious black cat.

Use your energy bar to speed up and see if you can use your cat to race its way to the top of the tree.
But watch out for the branches!
